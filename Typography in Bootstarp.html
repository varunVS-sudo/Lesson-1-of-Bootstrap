Football

Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. Unqualified, the word football is understood to refer to whichever form of football is the most popular in the regional context in which it appears. Sports commonly called football include association football (known as soccer in some countries), gridiron football (specifically American football or Canadian football), Australian rules football, rugby union and rugby league, and Gaelic football.
